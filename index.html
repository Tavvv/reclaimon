FAQ

Honest answers.

What is ReclaimOn?+

ReclaimOn is an iPhone and iPad screen time app that helps you reclaim time from distracting apps. You pick a goal, anchor it to a real time and a real place, and ReclaimOn shields the apps that pull you away for that window — every focused minute counts toward a weekly target.

How is ReclaimOn different from other app blockers?+

Most blockers only count the time you resisted. ReclaimOn anchors one goal to a real time and place, so shields switch on when you arrive and off when you leave — and it counts the minutes you actually put in. Honest post-session reflections replace streak guilt with coach insights about what's working.

Is ReclaimOn free?+

Yes — the full app is free for 21 days, the time it takes to build a habit, with no card and no account. After that you can stay on the free-forever tier (1 goal, 2 anchors, app shielding, joining Focus Pacts, and a Home Screen widget) or upgrade to ReclaimOn+ for $5.99/month or $29.99/year.

Can ReclaimOn see which apps I block?+

No. Apple's Screen Time API returns opaque tokens, so ReclaimOn can't see which apps you block or anything about your usage. Everything stays on your device — there's no account, no analytics, and no ads.

Does it work with Apple Health?+

Yes. With ReclaimOn+, Apple Health sync is read-only: workouts and mindful minutes logged in other apps like Strava, Calm, or Headspace count toward matching goals automatically. ReclaimOn never writes to Apple Health.
